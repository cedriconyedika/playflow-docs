# PlayFlow Dashboard Guide
**Simple Payment Processing for Small Businesses**

---

## Introduction

PlayFlow helps you accept payments from customers, track transactions, and manage refunds—all from a single dashboard.

You don’t need any technical skills to get started. If you can use a browser, you can use PlayFlow.

With PlayFlow, you can:
- Accept payments online
- Share payment links with customers
- Track all your transactions in real time
- Refund customers when necessary

---

## Creating an Account

Getting started takes just a few minutes.

### Step 1: Sign Up
- Go to the PlayFlow website
- Click **"Create Account"**
- Enter your:
  - Email address
  - Business name
  - Password

### Step 2: Verify Your Email
- Check your inbox for a verification email
- Click the confirmation link

### Step 3: Log In
- Return to the PlayFlow website
- Log in using your email and password

---

## Setting Up Your First Payment

You can create a payment link and start accepting money immediately.

### Step 1: Access the Dashboard
- After logging in, you’ll land on your dashboard

### Step 2: Create a Payment
- Click **"Create Payment"**
- Fill in the required details:
  - **Amount** (e.g. 5000 NGN)
  - **Customer Email**
  - **Description** (optional)

### Step 3: Generate Payment Link
- Click **"Create Payment"**
- A payment link will be generated

### Step 4: Share the Link
- Send the link to your customer via:
  - WhatsApp
  - Email
  - SMS

### Step 5: Receive Payment
- Once the customer pays, the transaction will update automatically

---

## Viewing Transactions

You can monitor all your payments from the dashboard.

### Step 1: Go to Transactions
- Click **"Transactions"** from the menu

### Step 2: Understand Statuses
Each transaction will show a status:
- **Pending** → Payment started but not completed
- **Successful** → Payment completed
- **Failed** → Payment did not go through

### Step 3: View Details
- Click on any transaction to see:
  - Payment amount
  - Customer details
  - Date and time
  - Transaction reference

### Step 4: Search and Filter
- Use filters to find specific transactions
- Search by email or reference

## Troubleshooting

Here are solutions to common issues.

---

### Payment Not Received

**Possible causes:**
- Customer hasn’t completed the payment
- Network delay

**What to do:**
- Ask the customer to confirm payment
- Check transaction status in dashboard
- Wait a few minutes and refresh

---

### Payment Showing as Failed

**Possible causes:**
- Insufficient funds
- Card declined
- Network issues

**What to do:**
- Ask the customer to try again
- Suggest a different payment method

---

### Didn’t Receive Verification Email

**What to do:**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes
- Click **"Resend Email"** on the login page

---

### Can’t Log In

**What to do:**
- Double-check your email and password
- Click **"Forgot Password"** to reset
